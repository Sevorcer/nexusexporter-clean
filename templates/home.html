Setup

Up and running in 5 minutes

No SDKs. No code. The Madden Companion App pushes data to your league. Bot does the rest.

1

Create your league

Sign in with Discord, name your league, and copy the unique export URL we generate for you.

2

Paste into Madden Companion

Open the official EA Madden Companion App, paste the export URL, and tap Export. Data lands instantly.

3

Add the Discord bot

Invite the NexusLeague bot to your server, link it to your league with one slash command, and it starts posting.
