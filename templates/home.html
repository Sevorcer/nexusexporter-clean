Features

Everything Your League Needs

📊

Live Standings

Automatically updated standings grouped by division with seeds, win %, and more. Always current after every export.

🗓️

Schedule Tracking

Full season schedule with week-by-week results. See scores, upcoming matchups, and playoff brackets.

👥

Roster Management

Complete rosters for every team with player ratings, positions, ages, and dev traits.

🏅

Stat Leaders

Top performers across passing, rushing, receiving, touchdowns, sacks, and interceptions.

🤖

AI Headlines

GPT-powered weekly headlines that recap your league's action and post automatically to Discord.

⚡

Discord Integration

All league data posted directly to your Discord server. Slash commands for on-demand stats.

Setup

Set Up in Minutes

1

Create Your League

Sign in with Discord and create a league on the dashboard. You'll get a unique API key.

2

Export from Madden

Use the NexusLeague companion app to export your franchise data. It sends everything to your league automatically.

3

Connect the Bot

Add the NexusLeague Discord bot to your server, link it to your league, and it starts posting updates instantly.

Discord Bot

Powerful Discord Commands

Control everything from Discord with slash commands.

/standings Show full league standings
/schedule week View games for any week
/roster Browse team rosters
/leaders passing Top passing stat leaders
/leaders rushing Top rushing stat leaders
/leaders receiving Top receiving stat leaders
/player search Look up any player's stats
/post standings Post standings to Discord channel
/post headline Generate & post AI headline
/post weekly_recap Post weekly recap
