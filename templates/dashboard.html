MULTI-LEAGUE SUPPORT

NexusLeague supports unlimited leagues per account. Each league gets its own API key, league ID, and bot configuration — perfect for commissioners running multiple franchises.

MADDEN COMPANION APP

  1. 1 Download the Madden NFL Companion App on your phone (iOS or Android).
  2. 2 Open the app and navigate to your Franchise / Connected Franchise league.
  3. 3 Go to Settings → Export and paste the following URL:
https://nexusexporter-clean-production.up.railway.app
  1. 4 Enable Auto-Export and set the export interval to after each sim.
  2. 5 Sim a week in Madden — data will automatically sync to your dashboard.
WHAT SYNCS AUTOMATICALLY
  • Team rosters & depth charts
  • League standings & records
  • Weekly schedule & scores
  • Passing, rushing & receiving stats
  • Pre-season, regular season & playoffs filtering
  • Re-export safe — duplicate data is ignored
WEEKLY EXPORT ROUTINE
  1. 1.Sim your week in Madden
  2. 2.Companion app auto-exports within seconds
  3. 3.Dashboard updates automatically
  4. 4.Run /create_weekly_channels in Discord
  5. 5.Bot posts standings, schedule & stats recap

ADD BOT TO DISCORD

  1. 1 Make sure you are the owner or admin of the Discord server you want to add the bot to.
  2. 2 Click the invite URL below or paste it into your browser:
https://discord.com/oauth2/authorize?client_id=YOUR_BOT_ID&scope=bot+applications.commands&permissions=8

Replace YOUR_BOT_ID with your actual Discord Application/Bot client ID.

  1. 3 Select your server from the dropdown and click Authorize.
  2. 4 Complete the CAPTCHA if prompted.
  3. 5 The bot will appear offline until you complete the Configure Bot step.
REQUIRED PERMISSIONS
  • Send Messages & Embed Links
  • Manage Channels (create weekly channels)
  • Read Message History
  • Use Slash Commands & Application Commands
  • Mention @everyone / @here (for announcements)

/CONFIG COMMAND REFERENCE

Run /config at any time in Discord to view your current bot configuration — league ID, channel bindings, and all optional settings.

Settings are saved per-server, so you can have different configs in multiple Discord servers.

WHAT EACH CHANNEL DOES
LOG CHANNEL
Receives import confirmations and error alerts when data syncs from the companion app.
LEADERS CHANNEL
Auto-posts weekly stat leaders (passing, rushing, receiving) after each sim.
NEWS CHANNEL
Used for AI-generated news recaps and trade announcements (requires OpenAI key).
